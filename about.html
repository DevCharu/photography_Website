About me

Greetings! I'm Malcolm Lismore, an enthusiastic freelance photographer based along Scotland's stunning North West coast. My photography tells stories of the rugged Scottish landscapes, diverse wildlife, and elegant coastal birds. For me, photography isn't just a job; it's an artistic expression that freezes moments in time. Specializing in nature, I bring together technical skill and creative vision in every photograph. While my passion is capturing Scotland's wild beauty, I also excel in transforming special occasions like weddings, portraits and events into timeless memories. Come along on this visual adventure, and let's craft something exceptional together.
